Preparation of compound 21-g.
2-Amino-5-fluorobenzoic acid (20 g, 129 mmol) was dissolved in acetic acid (250 mL) at 0°C, and then N-bromosuccinimide (25 g, 140 mmol) was added in batchesto the above solution. The mixture was stirred at room temperature for 16 hours and then filtered. The filter cake was washed with petroleum ether (100 mL × 3). The filter cake was dried in vacuo to deliver a white solid 21-g (18.8 g, yield: 62%), this product was used without further purification.
