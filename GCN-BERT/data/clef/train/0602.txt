Step 3: (S)-Piperidin-4-ylmethyl 2-hydroxy-2-(3-hydroxyphenyl)-2-phenylacetate hydrochloride
A solution of (S)-tert-butyl 4-((2-hydroxy-2-(3-hydroxyphenyl)-2-phenylacetoxy)methyl)piperidine-1-carboxylate (7.07 g, 16.01 mmol) in 1,4-dioxane (20 mL) was added with a solution of HCl in dioxane (4M, 80 mL) and the reaction mixture was stirred at room temperature for 90 minutes. The solvent was evaporated under reduced pressure to afford the title compound (5.9 g, 97%).
