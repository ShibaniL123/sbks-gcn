Example 2A
Methyl 4-aminocubane-1-carboxylate hydrochloride
185 mg (0.55 mmol, purity 82%) of the compound from Example 1A were initially charged in 3 ml of a 4 M hydrogen chloride solution in dioxane, and the mixture was stirred at RT overnight. The mixture was concentrated and the residue was dried under reduced pressure. This gave 141 mg (99% of theory, purity 99%) of the title compound
