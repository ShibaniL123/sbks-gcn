Step 4: Preparation of tert-butyl 4-(3-(3-(N-phenylpentaneamido)prop-1-ynyl(benzoyl)piperazine-1-carboxylate
A final product tert-butyl 4-(3-(3-(N-phenylpentaneamido)prop-1-ynyl)benzoyl)piperazine-1-carboxylate was obtained (323 mg, 70% yield) by the same method as described in Step 5 of Example 1 using the N-phenylpentaneamide (275 mg, 1.55 mmol) obtained in Step 1 of Example 1 and the tert-butyl 4-(3-(3-(methylsulfonyloxy)prop-1-ynyl)benzoyl)piperazine-1-carboxylate (982 mg, 2.33 mmol) obtained in Step 3.
