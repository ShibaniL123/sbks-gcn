PREPARATIVE EXAMPLE 3
Preparation of (S)-2-(1-aminoethyl)-5-chloro-3-(pyridine-3-yl)quinazoline-4(3H)-one
Step 1: Preparation of tert-butyl (S)-(1-(5-chloro-4-oxo-3-(pyridine-3-yl)-3,4-dihydroquinazoline-2-yl)ethyl)carbamate
4.06 g of tert-butyl (S)-(1-(5-chloro-4-oxo-3-(pyridine-3-yl)-3,4-dihydroquinazoline-2-yl)ethyl)carbamate was prepared as an ivory solid by using 1.59 g (16.90 mmol) of 3-aminopyridine according to the same manner as described in step 2 of Preparative Example 1 (10.14 mmol, yield: 60%).
