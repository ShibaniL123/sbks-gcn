To a solution of (±)-tert-butyl ((1 S*,2S*,5R*)-2-fluoro-5-hydroxycyclohexyl)carbamate (17b, 1.36 g, 5.83 mmol) in MeOH (20 mL) was added 4 M HCl in MeOH (20 mL, 80 mmol). The mixture was stirred at room temperature for 1 hour, then concentrated and lyophilized to give (±)-(1R*,3S*,4S*)-3-amino-4-fluorocyclohexan-1-ol hydrochloride (Intermediate 17, 0.985 g, 100%) as a white hygroscopic solid.
