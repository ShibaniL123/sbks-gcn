Step 2: Preparation of tert-butyl 4-(3-(3-hydroxyprop-1-ynyl)benzoyl)piperazine-1-carboxylate
Tert-butyl 4-(3-(3-hydroxyprop-1-ynyl)benzoyl)piperazine-1-carboxylate was obtained (2.32 g, 90% yield) by the same method as described in Step 3 of Example 1 using the tert-butyl 4-(3-bromobenzoyl)piperazine-1-carboxylate (2.75 g, 7.45 mmol) obtained in Step 1 and propargyl alcohol (0.87 ml, 14.89 mmol).
