Intermediate 16. 8-Bromo-6-chloro-9-(2-methoxyethyl)-9H-pyrido[3,4-b]indole
8-Bromo-6-chloro-9H-pyrido[3,4-b]indole (1.0 g, 3.55 mmol) in DMF (10 ml) was treated with cesium carbonate (3.4 g, 17.76 mmol) and 2-bromoethyl methyl ether (0.59 g, 4.26 mmol). The mixture was treated in an ultrasonic bath for 1 h and then stirred at room temperature for 3 days. The solid was filtered off with suction, and the filtrate was concentrated. The crude product was purified by preparative RP HPLC. The fractions containing the product were combined and concentrated, and the residue freeze-dried. 0.56 g (35%) of the title compound was obtained in the form of its salt with trifluoroacetic acid.
