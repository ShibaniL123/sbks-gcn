EDITORIAL NOTE 2015101258 There are 3 pages of Description which are not page numbered A Toothbrush Technical Field This utility model is a kind of toothbrush. Background Technology Toothbrush is one of common washing facilities. Most toothbrushes are used in coordination with cup. After used for a long time, the cup will generate dirt condensation at the bottom. It is very troublesome for cleaning. Moreover, toothbrush is only provided with a simple function of teeth brushing. It is unable to meet specific needs of actual use. Description on the Invention The technical issue to be solved by this utility model is to provide a kind of toothbrush. This device is of good skid resistance, which is convenient for holding. At the same time, this device can be used to clean the bottom of the cup with the cleaning sponge, so as to avoid dirt condensation, and improve sanitation. To solve the said issue, this utility model adopts the following technical scheme: This kind of toothbrush includes the toothbrush stem. An end is provided on the left side of the said toothbrush stem. Plug-in piece is coordinated on the said end. A toothbrush head is arranged on the said plug-in piece. The said toothbrush head is provided with brushing hair. A plug hole is provided on the right side of the said toothbrush stem. Cleaning sponge is provided in the said plug hole. Finger grooves are provided on the left side of the said toothbrush stem. The said finger grooves are rounded. Four finger grooves are provided in an annular form. Antiskid salient point is provided on the said toothbrush stem. As an optimized technical scheme, the said toothbrush stem is made of PVC plastics. The diameter of the said toothbrush stem is 8mm- 16mm. As an optimized technical scheme, it is of integral structure for the said end, toothbrush stem and plug-in piece. As an optimized technical scheme, an adhesive disc is provided on the outer surface close to the right end of the said toothbrush stem. As an optimized technical scheme, the said brushing hair is of nanometer hair. Beneficial effects of this utility model are described as follows: This device is of good skid resistance, which is convenient for holding. At the same time, this device can be used to clean the bottom of the cup with the cleaning sponge, so as to avoid dirt condensation, and improve sanitation. This device is featured by simple structure and low cost. It is suitable for promotion and wide application.
