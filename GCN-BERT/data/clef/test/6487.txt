A final product, N-(4-fluorophenyl)-N-(3-(3-(piperazine-1-carbonyl)phenyl)prop-2-ynyl)pentaneamide, was obtained (356mg, 58% yield) by the same method as described in Example 2 using the tert-butyl-4-(3-(3-(N-(4-fluorophenyl)pentaneamido)prop-1-ynyl)benzoyl)piperazine-1-carboxylate (762 mg, 1.46 mmol) obtained in Example 25.
