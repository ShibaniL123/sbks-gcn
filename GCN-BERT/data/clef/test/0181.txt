EXAMPLES
Example 1 Preparation of a compound of Formula I
Step 1: 4-chloro-7-fluoro-6-nitroquinazoline
7-fluoro-6-nitroquinazoline-4(3H)-one (2.0 g, 9.6 mmol) and one drop of N,N-dimethylformamide were refluxed overnight in thionyl chloride (6 mL), and then the reaction mixture was concentrated in vacuo. To the residue was added toluene, and then concentrated in vacuo again to remove the remaining thionyl chloride. The title compound (2 g, 92%) was obtained.
