Phenyl 3,4-O-isopropylidene-1-thio-α-L-arabinopyranoside, 17.
The synthesis follows a procedure similar to that described for the preparation of 16, starting with 15 (3.91 g, 16.15 mmol), 2,2-dimethoxypropane (120 mL, 969.00 mmol) and 10-camphorsulfonic acid (0.26 mg, 1.13 mmol), yielding product 17 (3.85 g, 13.63 mmol) as a white solid with a yield of 85%. P.f.: 92-93 °C.
