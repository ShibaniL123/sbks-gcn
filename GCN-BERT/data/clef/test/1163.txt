Preparation Example 6-5 : Preparation of 1-(1-methylpiperidin-4-yl)ethanone
1-(Piperidin-4-yl)ethanone hydrochloride (4.5 g, 8.32 mmol) was added to 30 ml of formic acid and 30 ml of formaldehyde and the mixture was stirred with reflux for 18 hrs. The solvent was concentrated under reduced pressure and to the solution were added hydrochloric acid and acetone. The resulting solid was filtered to give the white title compound (6.36 g, 95.0 %).
