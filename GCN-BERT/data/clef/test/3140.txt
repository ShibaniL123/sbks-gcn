40.2: 3-methyl-5-(2-methyl-pyridin-3-yl)-1H-pyrimidine-2,4-dione
Similarly to example 2.3, starting from 40 mg (0.1 mmol) of 1-benzhydryl-3-methyl-5-(2-methyl-pyridin-3-yl)-1H-pyrimidine-2,4-dione, 23 mg (100%) of 3-methyl-5-(2-methyl-pyridin-3-yl)-1H-pyrimidine-2,4-dione is obtained in the form of a yellow solid.
