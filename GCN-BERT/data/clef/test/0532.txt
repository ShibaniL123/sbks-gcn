Example 41. Preparation of ethyl 2-(4-(3-(N-phenylpentaneamido)prop-1-yn-1-yl)benzamido)acetate (LMT-1014)
Ethyl 2-(4-(3-(N-phenylpentaneamido)prop-1-yn-1-yl)benzamido)acetate was obtained (102.18 mg, 54% yield) by the same method as described in Example 39 using the 4-(3-(N-phenylpentaneamido)pro-1-yn-1-yl)benzoic acid (150.00 mg, 0.45 mmol) obtained in Example 38 and glycineethylester hydrochloride (93.70 mg, 0.67 mmol).
