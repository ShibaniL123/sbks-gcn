Preparation of 2-chloro-5-((1R,3R)-2,2-dichloro-3-(4-fluoro-3-(trifluoromethyl)phenyl)cyclopropane-1-carboxamido)-N-(2,4-difluoro-3-(phenylsulfonamido)phenyl)benzamide (F1295)
