Example Compound 23
2-((1-(2-aminophenyl)-3,5-dimethyl-1H-pyrazol-4-yl)amino)-2-oxoethyl 2-(2-methyl-1H-indol-1-yl)acetate
A suspension of 2-((3,5-dimethyl-1-(2-nitrophenyl)-1 H-pyrazol-4-yl)amino)-2-oxoethyl 2-(2-methyl-1 H-indol-1-yl)acetate (2.9 mg, 0.006 mmol) and 10% Pd/C (spatula tip) in EtOH (0.5 mL) was stirred under H2 (1 atm) for 18 h. The mixture was concentrated in vacuo and purified by column chromatography (3% MeOH in CH2Cl2) to give 2.3 mg (85%) the title compound
