Example 17. Preparation of N-(4-fluorophenyl)-N-(3-(4-(piperazine-1-carbonyl)phenyl)prop-2-ynyl)pentaneamide (LMT-840)
A final product, N-(4-fluorophenyl)-N-(3-(4-(piperazine-1-carbonyl)phenyl)prop-2-ynyl)pentaneamide, was obtained (1.06 g, 58% yield) by the same method as described in Example 12 using the tert-butyl 4-(4-(3-(N-(4-fluorophenyl)pentaneamido)prop-1-ynyl)benzoyl)piperazine-1-carboxylate (2.28 g, 4.37 mmol) obtained in Example 16.
