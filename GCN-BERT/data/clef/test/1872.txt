Step 2: Preparation of (S)-5-chloro-3-phenyl-2-(pyrrolidine-2-yl)quinazoline-4(3H)-one
3.3 g of (S)-5-chloro-3-phenyl-2-(pyrrolidine-2-yl)quinazoline-4(3H)-one was prepared as a beige solid by using 5.53 g (12.99 mmol) of tert-butyl (S)-2-(5-chloro-4-oxo-3-phenyl-3,4-dihydroquinazoline-2-yl)pyrrolidine-1-carboxylate prepared in step 1 according to the same manner as described in step 3 of Preparative Example 1 (10.13 mmol, yield: 78%).
