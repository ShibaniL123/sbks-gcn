THE PATENTS ACT, 1970 (39 OF 1970) & THE PATENTS RULES, 2003 COMPLETE SPECIFICATION (See Section 10; Rule 13) TITLE HOT-DIP GALVANIZED STEEL SHEET AND PRODUCTION METHOD THEREFOR APPLICANT JFE STEEL CORPORATION NATIONALITY: JAPAN OF 2-3, UCHISAIWAI-CHO 2-CHOME, CHIYODA-KU, TOKYO 1000011 JAPAN The following specification particularly describes the nature of the invention and the manner in which it is to be performed. [Claim 1]A hot-dip galvanized steel sheet comprising a steel sheet having a composition containing 0.100% to 0.200% C, 0.50% or less Si, 0.60% or less Mn, 0.100% or less P, 0.0100% or less S, 0.010% to 0.100% Al, and 0.0100% or less N on a mass basis, the remainder comprising Fe and inevitable impurities, the steel sheet having a microstructure containing a ferrite phase having an area fraction of 60% to 79%, a pearlite phase having an area fraction of 20% to 30%, and a bainite phase having an area fraction of 1% to 5%, the area fraction of a cementite phase present in a grain of the ferrite phase being 5% or less. [Claim 2]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 1, wherein the composition of the steel sheet further contains at least one selected from the group consisting of 0.05% to 0.80% Cr, 0.005% to 0.100% V, 0.005% to 0.500% Mo, 0.01% to 0.10% Cu, 0.01% to 0.10% Ni, and 0.0003% to 0.2000% B on a mass basis. [Claim 3]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 1, wherein the composition of the steel sheet further contains at least one selected from the group consisting of 0.001% to 0.005% Ca and 0.001% to 0.005% of an REM on a mass basis. [Claim 4]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 1, wherein the composition of the steel sheet further contains at least one selected from the group consisting of 0.05% to 0.80% Cr, 0.005% to 0.100% V, 0.005% to 0.500% Mo, 0.01% to 0.10% Cu, 0.01% to 0.10% Ni, and 0.0003% to 0.2000% B and at least one selected from the group consisting of 0.001% to 0.005% Ca and 0.001% to 0.005% of an REM on a mass basis. [Claim 5]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 1, being a hot-dip galvannealed steel sheet. [Claim 6]-The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 1, comprising a galvanizing layer, wherein the galvanizing layer is a hot-dip galvannealing layer. [Claim 7]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 6, wherein the galvannealing layer has an Fe content of 7% to 15%. [Claim 8]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 1, having a tensile strength of 440 MPa or more. [Claim 9]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 8, wherein the tensile strength is 440 MPa to 490 MPa. [Claim 10]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 1, having a stretch flangeability of 77% or more. [Claim 11]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 1, having an elongation of 35% or more. [Claim 12]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 1, wherein the content of Si is 0.01% to 0.50%. [Claim 13]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 1, wherein the content of Mn is 0.10% to 0.60%. [Claim 14]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet according to Claim 1, wherein the content of P is 0.003% to 0.100%. [Claim 15]A method for manufacturing a hot-dip galvanized steel sheet, comprising:preparing a steel material having a composition containing 0.100% to 0.200% C, 0.50% or less Si, 0.60% or less Mn, 0.100% or less P, 0.0100% or less S, 0.010% to 0.100% Al, and 0.0100% or less N on a mass basis, the remainder comprising Fe and inevitable impurities;heating the steel material;hot-rolling the steel material at a finishing delivery temperature not lower than the Ar3 temperature;coiling a hot-rolled sheet at a temperature of 600°C or lower;pickling the hot-rolled sheet;performing heating to a temperature of 650°C or higher at an average heating rate of 10 °C/s or more;performing holding at a temperature of 700°C to (Ac3 5)°C for ten seconds or more;performing cooling to a temperature of 300°C to 500°C at an average cooling rate of 10 °C/s to 200 °C/s;performing holding at a temperature of 300°C to 500°C for 30 seconds to 300 seconds; andperforming hot-dip galvanizing. [Claim 16]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet-manufacturing method according to Claim 15, further comprising cold-rolling the pickled hot-rolled sheet. [Claim 17]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet-manufacturing method according to Claim 16, wherein the cold-rolling reduction is 40% to 85%. [Claim 18]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet-manufacturing method according to Claim 15, further comprising performing alloying after hot-dip galvanizing. [Claim 19]The hot-dip galvanized steel sheet-manufacturing method according-to Claim 18, wherein alloying is performed by heating a steel sheet to a temperature of 450oC to 600°C.Dated this 2nd day of January 2014.
