Step 2: Preparation of (S)-5-chloro-3-(pyridine-3-yl)-2-(pyrrolidine-2-yl)quinazoline-4(3H)-one
2.6 g of (S)-5-chloro-3-(pyridine-3-yl)-2-(pyrrolidine-2-yl)quinazoline-4(3H)-one was prepared as a white solid by using 3.83 g (8.97 mmol) of tert-butyl (S)-2-(5-chloro-4-oxo-3-(pyridine-3-yl)-3,4-dihydroquinazoline-2-yl)pyrrolidine-1-carboxylate prepared in step 1 according to the same manner as described in step 3 of Preparative Example 1 (7.80 mmol, yield: 87%).
