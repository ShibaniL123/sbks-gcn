Step 2: Preparation of (4-(3-hydroxyprop-1-ynyl)phenyl)(piperidin-1-yl)methanone
(4-(3-hydroxyprop-1-ynyl)phenyl)(piperidin-1-yl)methanone was obtained (374 mg, yield 90%) by the same method as described in Step 3 of Example 1 using the (4-bromophenyl)(piperidine-1-yl)methanone (458.2 mg, 1.71 mmol) obtained in Step 1 and propargyl alcohol (0.199 ml, 3.42 mmol).
