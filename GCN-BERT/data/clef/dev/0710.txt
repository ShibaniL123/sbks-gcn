Example 14. Preparation of N-(3-fluorophenyl)-N-(3-(4-(piperazine-1-carbonyl)phenyl)prop-2-ynyl)pentaneamide (LMT-885)
A final product, N-(3-fluorophenyl)-N-(3-(4-(piperazine-1-carbonyl)phenyl)prop-2-ynyl)pentaneamide, was obtained (94 mg, 58% yield) by the same method as described in Example 12 using the tert-butyl 4-(4-(3-(N-(3-fluorophenyl)pentaneamido)prop-1-ynyl)benzoyl)piperazine-1-carboxylate (200 mg, 0.383 mmol) obtained in Example 13.
